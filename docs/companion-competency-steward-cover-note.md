## Rules to apply (authority order)

- **Theory Manual** governs meaning; **Operational Definitions Manual** governs exact terminology; **MKB v2.1** is the competency source of record.
- Assign the competency that matches the **developmental work the situation calls for**, not merely its topic. (Example: a situation *about* sex may develop a Communication or Trust competency rather than a Physical Intimacy one — choose by the skill being built.)
- One **primary** competency per situation (the workbook has an optional secondary column if you want to note a runner-up).
- If two competencies fit equally, prefer the one whose domain matches the situation's primary consumer category.
